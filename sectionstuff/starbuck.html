Starbuck

Starbuck... where do I even begin?

The best fighter pilot aboard the last remaining Battlestar in humanity after the attack of the Cylons.

Also known as Kara Thrace, she grows across the course of the years spent in space, searching for the fabled planet known as "Earth," and eventually becomes someone willing to sacrifice everything for the betterment of humanity.

She's also a badass.
